Attendance

At least 75% needed to receive credit. It is also really important that your youth are on time for lessons every day. If late, minutes will be added up until 50 and youth will need to do a makeup lesson for those. They also need to stay above 75% all semester and are able to do makeup lessons as needed.

Makeup lessons

Here are their options for makeup lessons:

  • Canvas Student (30 minutes per lesson online)
  • Home Study Manual (30 minutes per lesson with notes turned in)
  • General Conference (1 seminary day for each session watched and notes turned in)

Parent's role in seminary

Parents have the primary responsibility to teach their children the gospel of Jesus Christ; and to answer their doctrinal questions. Church leaders assist parents in this responsibility. Seminary teachers primarily assist parents by teaching students the gospel of Jesus Christ as found in the scriptures and the words of the prophets, emphasizing the doctrinal importance of the family and the high priority that family members and family activities deserve.
